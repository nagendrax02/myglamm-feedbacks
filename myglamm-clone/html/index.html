SHOP BY SKIN TONE
Light Skin
Light skin tones are the perfect canvas for everything from pastels to pop shades. A little bit of blush, a little bit of warmth and a hint of glow is all you need to enhance your natural skin tone.
Medium Skin
Medium skin has just the right balance of warm and golden undertones to give off a natural, almost sun-kissed glow. Play up your gorgeous olive skin with peaches, corals, reds, metallics and earthy shades!
Dusky Skin
Deep and dusky skin can pull off just about any shade. Use that to your advantage with jewel tones like ruby, amethyst and emerald, as well as radiant contrasting hues like cool aquas and snowy whites to make you stand out.
